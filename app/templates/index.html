HOW IT WORKS.

No pricking! Just a photo of the eye.

REQUEST YOUR DIAGNOSIS

The diagnoser will require your permission in order to take a photo of your eye to determine whether you may be suffering from diabetes or not.

GET DIAGNOSED

Request to be diagnosed by a diagnoser in your area. The diagnoser will come to you and do the diagnosis where you are or opt to take the tests at specified locations.

BOOK YOUR APPOINTMENT

Book an appointment with a doctor if the result of your diagnosis shows that you may be diabetic.
